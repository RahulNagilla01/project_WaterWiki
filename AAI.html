Overview

Agriculture is the backbone of our economy, and irrigation plays a critical role in sustaining agricultural productivity. Efficient irrigation techniques ensure that crops receive the necessary water supply without wastage, leading to better yields and sustainable farming practices.

Irrigation Techniques

There are various irrigation techniques used in agriculture, including surface irrigation, drip irrigation, and sprinkler irrigation. Each method has its advantages and is suited to different types of crops and geographical conditions.

Sustainability

Sustainable irrigation practices help in conserving water and maintaining soil health. Techniques like drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting are essential for reducing water usage and ensuring that agricultural practices do not deplete natural resources.
